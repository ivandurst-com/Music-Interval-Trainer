Instructions

First, create a chord progession by selecting a chord from the "Select Chord..." dropdown list, and clicking "Add." You can make the progression as long or as short as you'd like, and you can loop it by clicking the "Loop" checkbox.

Next, select a division from the "Select Division..." dropdown list ("whole" is a good starting point), adjust the tempo, and click "Play." If you'd like to hide the current note from view, uncheck the "Show Current Note" checkbox.

Have fun!
